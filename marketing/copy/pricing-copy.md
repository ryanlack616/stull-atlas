# Pricing Copy

## Tier Descriptions

### Free — $0/forever
**For curious potters exploring glaze chemistry.**
Browse the Stull chart, calculate UMF, and look up materials — no sign-up required.

### Solo — $8/month
**For studio potters who want to go deeper.**
Save recipes, calculate line blends, import/export your data, and get limit warnings when something looks off.

### Pro — $18/month  
**For serious glaze development.**
Full blend suite (triaxial, biaxial, radial, quadaxial), genetic optimizer, AI suggestion engine, space-filling experimental designs, and advanced analysis tools.

### Edu — Contact us
**For classrooms and workshops.**
Everything in Pro, plus classroom management, shared recipe collections, and volume pricing. Contact us for a quote.

## FAQ Copy

**Can I try Pro for free?**
Yes! Sign up and get 7 days of Pro automatically. NCECA attendees get 30 days — ask us at the booth.

**What happens when my trial ends?**
You keep your saved recipes and drop to the Free tier. Upgrade anytime to unlock Pro features again.

**Can I cancel anytime?**
Absolutely. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Your data stays yours.

**Do you offer student discounts?**
Yes — Edu pricing is available for accredited programs. Individual students can use Solo at full price or ask their instructor about a classroom account.

**Is my data private?**
Your recipes are stored encrypted and never shared. We don't sell data or run ads. See our privacy policy.
