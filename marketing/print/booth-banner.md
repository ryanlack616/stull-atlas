- **Size**: 33" × 80" (standard retractable)
- **Resolution**: 150 DPI minimum
- **File format**: PDF/X-1a or high-res PNG

### Table Banner
- **Size**: 72" × 30" (6ft table drape front)
- **Resolution**: 100 DPI minimum
