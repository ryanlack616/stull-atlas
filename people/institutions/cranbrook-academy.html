Cranbrook Academy of Art

institution 41 connections
About
Graduate-only MFA in Bloomfield Hills, MI (25 min from Detroit). Curriculum-free, studio-based. Ceramics program has Dutch Blaauw gas kilns, 3D clay printer, Soldner mixers. Notable ceramics alumni include Jun Kaneko and Donald Lipski ('73).
