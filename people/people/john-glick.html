John Glick

person 18 connections Est. 1938
About
Renowned potter, Plum Tree Pottery, Farmington Hills (1964-2016). Born 1938 Detroit. Wayne State BFA 1960, Cranbrook MFA 1962 under Grotell. 33+ artists-in-residence over 50 years. ~300,000 pieces. Abstract Expressionist. Work in Smithsonian, DIA, LACMA, Museum of Arts and Design, Yale. Died April 6 2017.
