Peter Pincus NCECA 2026

person 33 connections Est. 1982
About
Ceramic artist known for nerikomi (colored clay) techniques creating intricate geometric patterns. BFA Kansas City Art Institute, MFA Alfred University. Resident Artist Director at the Ceramic Shop at Rochester Contemporary Art Center. Known for combining historical ceramic forms with contemporary color and pattern work.
