10. Talking Points & Common Questions

"How is this different from Glazy?"

"Glazy is the database ‚Äî Derek Au did amazing work making it open. Stull Atlas is a different lens on that data ‚Äî interactive Stull charts, blend calculators, an optimizer, AI suggestions. They're complementary."

"How is this different from Insight?"

"Insight is recipe-level calculation. Stull Atlas starts from the other direction ‚Äî see the whole landscape first, then drill into recipes. Plus blend calculators and AI suggestions are unique."

"Is this free?"

"The explorer, UMF calculator, materials database, and guide are always free. Blend calculators, optimizer, and AI suggestions are Pro ‚Äî $18/month or $29 for 3 months NCECA deal."

"Who built this?"

"I did ‚Äî I'm Ryan Lack, a potter in Howell, Michigan, about an hour from here. I wanted a better way to visualize glaze chemistry, and this is what happened."

"Where does the data come from?"

"9,000+ recipes from Glazy ‚Äî Derek Au's incredible open database. Materials data from Tony Hansen's Digitalfire Reference Library."

Messaging Rules

  • Always complementary to Glazy and Insight ‚Äî never competitive
  • Never say "disrupt" ‚Äî we're serving a community
  • Don't oversell AI ‚Äî constraint optimization with NLP (but it feels like magic)
  • Don't call it "the best" ‚Äî call it "a new way to see glaze chemistry"
