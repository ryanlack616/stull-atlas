About
Distinguished Professor of Art at Penn State University. Major functional potter. Work explores simplicity, function, and the relationship between art and everyday life. Multiple NEA and other national awards. Widely exhibited and published. Exhibiting in 'Mom's Collection' at N'Namdi Gallery, NCECA 2026.
